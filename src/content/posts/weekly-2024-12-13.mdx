List of interesting things I read or learnt this week.

## Tools
Over the last week, I've added a bunch of tools to my shell to
make things easier.

- [eza](https://github.com/eza-community/eza): `ls` replacement, honestly I just like the nicer colors and icons
- [bat](https://github.com/sharkdp/bat): `cat` replacement
- [delta](https://github.com/dandavison/delta): nicer diffs
- [zoxide](https://github.com/ajeetdsouza/zoxide): nicer `cd`
- [tlrc](https://github.com/tldr-pages/tlrc): tldr client
- [lazygit](https://github.com/jesseduffield/lazygit): terminal git client
- [fd](https://github.com/sharkdp/fd): find replacement 
- [fzf](https://github.com/junegunn/fzf): fuzzy finder

## Astro 5
[Astro 5](https://astro.build/blog/astro-5/) is out! As of writing this post, this website has been updated to Astro 5.

## Willow: New Quantum Chip by Google
Google has announced their new [quantum computer chip](https://blog.google/technology/research/google-willow-quantum-chip/). Very interesting, could spell the doom of existing encryption techniques.
